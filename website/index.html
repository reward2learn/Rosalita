Staff Costs

54-68% → 22% of revenue · IDR 50-80M/mo

Reduce core FTE from 22 → 16 (waitstaff 5→4, bar 3→2, kitchen 6→5)

Use part-time/casual staff for Fri-Sat peaks (pay per shift, no fixed cost)

Implement 7shifts AI scheduling to predict staffing needs from sales data

Cross-train staff for multi-role capability (FOH can bar, kitchen can prep)

Hold weekly 15-min standup to maintain morale through restructuring

Track hours worked vs. revenue daily — flag any day above 25% labor ratio

+
2

Menu Fixes

60 → 48 items, remove Asian Corner, fix promos · IDR 10-20M/mo

Remove Asian Corner (6 items: Nasi Goreng, Mie Ayam, Tom Yam Gai, etc.) — brand mismatch

Remove duplicate Chicken Wings listings (keep smoked + fried option only)

Merge Nachos & Mexi Fries into one menu line (“choose your base: chips or fries”)

Fix Monday: replace 25% off with “Buy 1 Main, Get 1 Dessert for 20K” bundle

Fix Wednesday: replace 67% off wings with “6 Wings + Beer for 89K” bundle

Reduce taco/burrito fillings from 7 to 4-5 based on 4-week POS sales data

Increase prices on underpriced items: Chicken Fingers 59K→68K, Cobb Salad 99K→108K, Churros 49K→55K

Revenue Windows

Happy Hour, Brunch, Late-night · IDR 50-100M/mo

Launch daily Happy Hour (4-7PM): 20% off cocktails, half-price appetizers, beer IDR 35K

Partner with 5 nearby 5-star hotels: concierge referrals with 10% guest discount

Track revenue by time period weekly — cut any window not hitting minimum covers

Phase 1: Stop the Bleeding — July – September 2026

Zero cost

Immediate cost structure fixes that require no investment. Sorted by revenue impact, highest first.

  • Fix loss-leading promotions (Monday 25% off → bundle, Wednesday 67% off wings → wing+beer) IDR 10-18M/mo
  • Complete staff restructuring: 22 → 16 core FTE + casuals for peaks IDR 13.5M/mo
  • Lock in lower rent at IDR 30M/month through December 2027 up to IDR 13M/mo
  • Deploy free AI tools (ChatGPT, Canva, Meta Business Suite) for marketing IDR 5-10M/mo
  • Menu cleanup: remove Asian Corner, duplicate Chicken Wings, merge Nachos & Mexi Fries Enabling
  • Analyse 4-week sales mix to identify low performers Data
  • Track profitability by day of week to evaluate promotions Data
+

Phase 2: Menu & Revenue Optimization — October – December 2026

IDR 130-210M/mo

Requires sales mix data from Phase 1 and some investment in menu redesign and staff training. Sorted by revenue impact, highest first.

  • Upselling training for staff: 3-step upsell (starter, drink pairing, dessert) IDR 60M/mo
  • Launch weekend brunch Sat-Sun 10AM-2PM (Build-Your-Own-Taco bar + bottomless drinks) IDR 30-50M/mo
  • Selective price increases on underpriced items (Chicken Fingers, Cobb Salad, Churros, etc.) IDR 20-30M/mo
  • Launch daily Happy Hour 4-7PM (20% off cocktails, half-price apps, beer specials) IDR 15-30M/mo
  • Hotel concierge partnerships with 5 five-star hotels within 1km IDR 10-20M/mo
  • Menu pruning based on 4-week sales data: 62 → 48-52 items IDR 5-15M/mo
  • Cocktail menu pricing & optimization (document recipes, create Margarita Flight) IDR 5-10M/mo
  • Implement 7shifts AI scheduling to reduce labor cost IDR 4-7M/mo
  • Introduce combo bundles (Lunch Deal, Rosalita’s Fiesta, Date Night) Enabling

IDR 27-48M/mo
